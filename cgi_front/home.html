About LWCGI

The Vision of the LW CGI is to promote sustained spiritual and numerical growth in our churches, commensurate with the expectations of the Ministry and the urgency of our Kingdom Agenda.

LW CGI will work with nominated Churches to deploy specific, strategic and tactical intervention for sustained growth; through focused and intense engagement with the Church leadership and brethren, by a select Team of Pastors equipped to produce the desired outcome.
